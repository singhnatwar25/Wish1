Here are the frequently asked questions that people enquired online before choosing a virtual office. We have gathered the most crucial ones and answer here to help you in making the right decision.

What is a virtual office?

A virtual office is a popular remote workspace model which offers a professional business address. It comes with various office-related facilities and services. From mail handling to reception facilities and access to meeting rooms, various office facilities are provided without having a physical office setup.

What are the types of virtual offices?

There are mainly three types of Virtual Offices-

Virtual Office for Company Registration
Virtual Office for Mail Handling
Virtual Office for GST Registration

What are the advantages of having a virtual office address?

There are so many advantages of having a virtual office in this city, some of them are listed below-

Zero furniture required
Access to Global Talent
Increased Productivity
No daily commuting to an office for work
Zero Utility Payments
Zero hardware cost
No hefty office leases
Liberty and Flexibility in work style etc.

What type of businesses opt for virtual offices?

From a real estate business, boutique, courier company to legal firm, and creative agencies etc. Several types of businesses use a virtual office space including freelancers, business owners, young entrepreneurs, SMEs etc.

What facilities do you get when you book a virtual office?

Following are the state-of-the-art facilities offered by a virtual office in this city-

Envelope Opening Service
Mailing Address
Video Conferencing Facilities
Meeting Space
Business Telephone Number
Scanning Service
Administrative Staff
Phone Answering Services
Professional Business Address etc.

What documents are required while booking a virtual office space?

Following are the required documents to be submitted by the companies which are incorporated-

Directors or Trustees ID Proof
Organization Incorporation Certificate
PAN Card

Following are the required documents to be submitted by the companies which are not incorporated-
